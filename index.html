The humanoids behind Giesela believe in developing unique open-source applications, while always* having fun!

Giesela was designed with the aim to solve a seemingly simple question: how to make the experience of listening to music on Discord effortless, using elements that are familiar to most users today, and presenting them in a way that 'just clicks' with the everyday user. We've spent over a year working on the brainchild that is Giesela, and we're super excited as we get closer to release! Expect something proper here soon™!
